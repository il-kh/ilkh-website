Inros Lackner (Cambodia) Co., Ltd. was engaged by the LEADER WIND ENERGY (CAMBODIA) CO., LTD. to carry out geotechnical investigations by Cone Penetration Tests (CPTu) for a Wind Turbines Project in Mondulkiri province. 

The investigations were carried out in two parts.

* Part I: Ground investigations by Cone Penetration Tests (CPTu),
* Part II: Ground investigations by borehole exploration.

The ground investigations by Cone Penetration Testing were carried out in nine (9) locations from 12th and 14th May 2025. [](<>)
