Residence 90 is a residential development consisting of 90 villas on a 2.7-hectare land at Phnom Penh City Centre. Inros Lackner Cambodia in association with Inros Lackner Vietnam developed the architectural, civil, structural & MEP design for the villas and the infrastructure design for the land, including roads, drainage, power and water supply, and wastewater treatment.

**Scope:**
Scope: Architectural, civil, structural & MEP design for following project stages:
- Concept review,
- Schematic and detailed design.
