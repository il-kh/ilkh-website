Our Advanced Load Instrumentation & Monitoring service provides precise measurement and monitoring solutions for structural loads, deformations, and forces in civil engineering projects. Using state-of-the-art equipment such as load cells, strain gauges, hydraulic cylinders, and dial gauges, we ensure accurate data collection for applications like pile load tests, excavation bracing, and tunnel support monitoring. This service is essential for assessing structural performance, ensuring safety, and optimizing designs in complex engineering environments. With reliable instrumentation and expert analysis, we deliver actionable insights to support informed decision-making and project success.

## Equipment
- Load Cells
  - 1 Geokon vibrating wire load cells model 4900 with field PC readout model GK-405
  - Monitors loads in tie-backs and rock bolts in the walls of excavations
  - Long-term loads in concrete dam tie-downs
  - Loads in steel arch tunnel supports
  - Pile load tests and loads in cross lot struts
  - 1 Omega through hole compression load cell model LC8400
  - Determine loading forces on large bolts or other suspended load applications 
  - 9 Geokon terminal boxes model 4999
  - Manual switch type terminal box
  - Connect a readout box ( e.g. model GK-405) to a mutiplicity of vibrating wire sensors
- Strain Gauge
  - 119 Geokon arc weldable strain gauges model 4000 and Omega strain meter model DP41-S
  - Arc weld to steel structures such as tunnel linings, excavation bracing, piles and bridges
  - 150 mm gauge length
  - 3000 µε range and 1 µε sensitivity
- Hydraulic Cylinder
  - 1 Larzep single acting high tonnage cylinders model SSR22010
  - Load return
  - Capacity: 220 tonnes
  - For weighing, lifting and positioning applications in civil engineering
  - 1 HHYG-50D single acting multi stage telescopic low-height hydraulic cylinder
  - Capacity: 50 tonnes
- Dial gauge
  - 4 Käfer M 2/50 S precision dial gauges
  - Scale: 0.01 mm
  - Range: 50 mm
