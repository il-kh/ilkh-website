We deliver advanced Building Information Modeling (BIM) services using Allplan software to create intelligent 3D models for geotechnical and civil engineering projects. Our BIM solutions integrate geotechnical data, structural elements, and construction sequences into coordinated digital models that enhance project visualization and coordination. We develop detailed 3D models of foundations, retaining structures, tunnels, and ground improvement works that facilitate clash detection and constructability reviews. Our BIM workflows improve communication between project stakeholders, reduce design conflicts, and support quantity takeoffs and construction planning. We provide model-based deliverables that enable seamless data exchange and support digital project delivery throughout the construction lifecycle.

## Software
- Allplan (BIM and 3D modeling platform)
- Revit
- Advanced modeling tools for geotechnical and civil structures
- Clash detection and coordination capabilities
- Quantity takeoff and documentation tools
